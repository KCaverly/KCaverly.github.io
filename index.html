alphatext

How can we understand and identify thematic relevance for companies in our universe?

Built a semantic search pipeline for North American equity filings. Managing everything from parsing, data storage, embedding generation, query inference and vector search. Leveraged HuggingFace, Databricks, Milvus and Kubernetes. Resulting in sub-second response times for top-k searches on 65M vectors.

mew

How can we systematically understand the narratives affecting the investments we are interested in?

Led development of a large scale NLP product, leveraging transformer models and Machine Readable News to identify and organize the way companies in our portfolio and investment universe are being discussed globally. Bespoke HuggingFace models were fine-tuned for Sentiment, Uncertainty, Novelty, Regression and Named Entity Recognition. Responsible for product direction and technical development of the modelling engine, data ingest pipeline, and API serving.
